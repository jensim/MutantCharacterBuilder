Privacy policy

User data

I will not give out or by any means share private information given me by you directly or by trusted party, if not forced by law.
However, It is my goal not to keep user information in the system, unless I absolutely have to.
I keep no passwords stored, and I require no names, I prefer it that way.

Oauth tokens

When signing in with a user account from a trusted party, Rollspelsportalen is given an access_token,
if the Oauth provider you signed in with has support for ending access_token life, I end it on
active logout from the site. Otherwise you end the token life through the providers own web interface.
