Character Builder toolkit

This started out as a project for generating characters, and maintaining these.

The project has always been driven by my need to evolve as a programmer and thuss been afflicted by my current cravings to learn new technologies.

My underlying objective through it all is my love to Swedish old style role playing games, those where you play by speaking your mind and rolling dice!
Personally, I'm not much of a dice rolling man, I tend to rely on facts, figures and solid arguments, and that might shine through for those out there who are dedicated chance-junkies.
